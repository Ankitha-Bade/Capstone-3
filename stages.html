TAEKWONDO STAGES


Each belt represents a level of achievment through your progress in Taekwondo. There are ten color belts before attaining a black belt, and nine degrees of black belt, as listed below.




1) Beginner ➡ White belt, Yellow-Stripe
2) Intermediate ➡ Yellow Belt, Green-Stripe, Green Belt
3) Advanced ➡ Blue-Stripe, Blue Belt, Red-Stripe, Red Belt
4) Junior Black-Belt ➡ Red-Stripe, 1, 2, 3 degree of Black Belt
5) Master ➡ 4, 5, 6 degree of Black Belt
6) Grand Master ➡ 7, 8, 9 degree of Black Belt
